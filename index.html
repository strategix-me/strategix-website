Welcome to Strategix Data & Projects

Strategix Data & Projects was born from the vision of creating a professional platform in Rwanda that empowers organizations through data-driven decision making. The idea emerged from the recognition that many businesses, NGOs, and government projects need expert guidance to utilize data effectively and manage projects efficiently. Founded by MUHIRE Edmond and a team of passionate experts, the company merges applied statistics, project management, and workforce development into an integrated solution for impactful results.

At Strategix Data & Projects, we believe in partnership, innovation, and excellence. We welcome collaborators, investors, and stakeholders to join us in this journey of transforming data and projects into real-world impact across Rwanda and beyond.

Our Vision

To become Rwanda's leading hub for data analytics, project excellence, and human capital development, shaping a smarter, more efficient, and impactful future by enabling organizations and communities to unlock their full potential.

Our Mission

To provide innovative solutions that transform data into actionable insights, build skilled and motivated teams, and optimize project execution for businesses, NGOs, and government initiatives, empowering organizations to make evidence-based decisions and achieve sustainable growth.
